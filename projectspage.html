JavaScript Games: Roll the Dice

JavaScript game built as part of the Udemy course: The Complete JavaScript Course 2021: From Zero to Expert! taught by Jonas Schmedtmann. Two player game, where each player rolls dice and adds the number displayed to their count while trying to reach 100. If either player rolls a "1", their count is reduced to "0". Players have the option to Hold their current count, in order to better avoid rolling a "1", but this allows the other player to take a turn.
